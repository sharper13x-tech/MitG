Jenny in a Bottle

Some stories find you when you need them most.

Grandmothers can forget things. Most of the time, what your grandmother forgets to tell you is pretty normal. Sometimes it's a magic ship arriving in the town graveyard, pursued by the King of Death… and the ship thinks you are its captain… but you're not… or at least you don't think you are. That's what is happening to Jenny in Book 1. It get's much weirder from there.

Jenny in a Bottle is an all-ages fantasy series for readers who want genuine stakes, a world that keeps getting bigger, and characters who earn everything they get. Adventure, mythology, fantasy, found family, and some genuinely scary moments — written to be read under the covers by kids or grownups who love a good story. Fans of Percy Jackson, The Chronicles of Narnia, Harry Potter, and Lemony Snicket will feel right at home.
